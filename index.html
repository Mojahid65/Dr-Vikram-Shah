About Dr. Vikram Shah

Dr. Vikram Shah is a renowned Ayurveda practitioner with over 21 years of experience in treating patients from around the world. He holds a Bachelor's degree in Ayurvedic Medicine and Surgery (BAMS) and is a certified Panchakarma specialist.

Dr. Shah's approach combines traditional Ayurvedic wisdom with modern healthcare understanding, creating personalized treatment plans that address the root cause of health issues rather than just symptoms.

He has successfully treated over 5000 patients from 25+ countries, helping them achieve better health through Ayurvedic principles, diet modifications, herbal remedies, and lifestyle changes.
